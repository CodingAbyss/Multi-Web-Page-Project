Sauteing
  • It is performed over a burner in a hot, shallow pan
  • Saute is a french word that translates to "jump"
  • Before doing anything make sure the oiled pan is hot before adding any food
Poaching
  • Is a gentle method of cooking
  • Foods are submerged in hot liquid between 140 degrees and 180 degrees
  • Low heat work especially well for delicate items, moisture, and flavor
Stewing
  • The result is a thick flavorful gravy filled with tender bites of meat and vegetables
  • Best foods to use when stewing is meats and vegetables
  • Foods are completely submerged in hot liquid while stewing
Baking
  • Baking uses indirect heat to surround foods and cook all sides
  • Baking is usually performed at lower temperatures than roasting
  • best foods to use is bked goods and pizza
Roasting
  • Performed inside an oven and uses indirect heat that cooks from all side for even browning
  • For tender cuts have the temperature up 450 degrees
  • Roasting can b performed at very low temperatures between 200 degrees and 350 degrees
Simmering
  • Is a gentle cooking method
  • This temperature range lies below the boiling point and produces tiny bubbles
  • To achieve a simmer, bring the water to the boiling point and then lower the temperature
